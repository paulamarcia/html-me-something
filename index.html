We Love To Travel to Show Our Pride

  • NYC
  • Las Vegas
  • Atlanta

NYC

My partner and I like to travel to NYC every year for their Pride activities in June. We've attended parades that have lasted 6hrs. We've seen celebrities from all over.

Las Vegas

Another one of our yearly trips to show our Pride is to Las Vegas in March. Thousands of people come from all over the world to celebrate in this marvelous city in the desert

Atlanta

Atlanta is a great city to celebrate anything but it is particularly exciting to participate in Black Pride events typically held around Labor Day weekend every year.
